How to Play Offroad Jeep Game Simulator

In Offroad Jeep Game Simulator, your goal is to navigate your jeep through rough off-road terrain and park it in the designated parking spot. Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your jeep, and use the spacebar to apply the handbrake. Be careful not to damage your jeep or get stuck in the terrain.

As you progress through the levels, the terrain becomes more challenging, with steeper hills, deeper mud, and more obstacles to overcome. The game features realistic jeep physics, so you'll need to adjust your driving style to handle the rough terrain. Challenge yourself to complete each level with minimal damage to your jeep to earn a high score.
